Display

Fill screen stretches the art edge-to-edge (cropping the sides) instead of letterboxing it — best for an immersive view on a phone. Fullscreen hides the browser chrome where the browser allows it; on iPhone, install to the Home Screen for the same effect.

Pattern regime (presets)

Reaction

Look

Switch between the glossy Ferrofluid look and the original black & white modes any time. Classic B&W = Filled with the regime field off.

Ferrofluid material

Lights the pattern as a glossy raised liquid with wet reflective beads, a backlit glow and a slowly drifting hue. Threshold & Edge/width above set how fat the fluid tubes are.

Colour pickup

The fluid picks up & blends a range of hues around the selected Palette as it moves (drive it with Flow). Spread = how far the hues wander from the palette (0 = palette only, high = rainbow). Ferrofluid mode.

Regime field (density)

Makes different areas sit in different regimes (thick worms, fine spots, bubbly holes) in one frame. Pick a paint mode, then drag on the canvas.

Motion

Tap any ~ beside a slider to give that parameter an LFO (rate, depth, offset, waveform) — the value sweeps on its own. Tap the LFO params header to collapse a strip while it keeps running. Morph is a one-tap preset: phase-offset LFOs on Feed & Kill.
Push the fluid in a direction so it flows and drags into combs — like tilting, but works on desktop too. Drives the colour pickup.
On a phone, tilt to make the fluid flow and the highlights slosh like real liquid. Tap Enable tilt first (iOS asks permission). Works best in Ferrofluid mode.

Seed / canvas

Drag on the canvas to draw/seed.

Record video

Records the canvas to recording.webm at 60fps. Run ./assemble.sh to turn it into a .mov matching the source (1260×1746).
